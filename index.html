About Saphir Group

Located in Makepe, Douala, Saphir Group is your premier destination for luxury event planning. We specialize in creating memorable weddings, birthday celebrations, and corporate events in our elegant reception hall.

Our experienced team ensures stress-free, perfectly executed events with personalized decoration, custom catering, and professional photo & video coverage. Every detail is carefully planned to transform your vision into reality.

With capacity for 250-400 guests, our modern venue combines elegance with functionality, providing the perfect backdrop for your most precious moments.
